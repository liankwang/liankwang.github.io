Lian Wang

lianw (at) sas (dot) upenn (dot) edu

I'm a first-year PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Pennsylvania, where I'm advised by Charles Yang. Prior to Penn, I got my BA in Linguistics and MS in Statistics from Stanford University, where I worked with Marc Feldman. I'm interested in language learning and change.
